The Commercial Model

How everyone
gets paid.

A performance-based structure where every party earns on real results — not promises, impressions, or upfront fees.

01

20% commission on tracked sales

GoWish earns 20% on every sale attributed through the platform. No flat fees, no minimum guarantees. The celebrity-brand only pays when GoWish delivers paying customers. Full stop.

02

GoWish funds & runs paid social

Meta and TikTok ad spend is covered entirely by GoWish. We create, run, and optimise the campaigns. The celebrity-brand provides likeness approval — GoWish does the rest. Zero ad budget required from the brand.

03

Celebrity licenses likeness for ads

The celebrity grants GoWish a limited licence to use their image and video in campaign ads. As the founder of the brand, this is simply authentic storytelling — not a paid endorsement performance. Usage rights are scoped, time-bound, and agreed per campaign.
